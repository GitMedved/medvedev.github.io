Why I decided to become a QA

Mar 1, 2025

I decided to become a QA because, for me, it's important not just to create something new, but to ensure that it works flawlessly. From the very beginning of my journey, I realized that quality is not just a stage in the development process, but a philosophy that requires attention to detail, patience, and a deep understanding of both the technical and user sides of a product. I enjoy being the one who guards quality, who identifies weaknesses and helps make the product better. For me, QA is not just about finding bugs; it's an opportunity to influence the final outcome so that users receive something that truly works and benefits them. I always strive to ensure that every detail is thought through and every scenario is tested. This is my way of making the world of technology a little bit better.
